About Me

Rachel is a New York based photographer who is currently pursuing a Bachelor in Fine Arts in Boston. She began her involvement in the artistic field in the seventh grade and has had her work curated into several galleries and shows. She has been an entrant in the LIAA Scholar-Artist Awards and has been given her high school chapter of National Art Honor Society's photographer recognition award. Rachel has also served as the president of her high school's photography club and film club. She has studied photography and graphic design at both the Fashion Institute of Technology and the School of Visual Arts in Manhattan. In addition, she has studied the arts at Penn State University's portfolio building program. She has worked as a photographer's assistant at several professional events and has taken corporate headshots, in addition to her own personal work. Rachel has recently served as the staff photographer at a local fire department's day camp and is currently the official photographer of her university's Student Government Association.
