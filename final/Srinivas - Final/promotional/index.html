Portfolio - Shriya

Promotional

In the 2017-2018 year, I was the Media Specialist for UTSAV, the South Asian Organization at Northeastern University. In this position, I did a lot of promotional work for vaious events. They ranged from Club Cards to Social Media Artwork to TShirts. The main ones are pictured below.

Dhol Baaje

This is a religious related activity called Garba that ahppens every fall. Everyone gets dressed up in traditional clothing and dances around the Goddess. Pictured here are the Club Card and Facebook Header.

Diwali/Eid Dinner

This was a formal dinner event where we served Indian food and celebrated the many religious holidays around Fall. Pictured here are the Club Card and Facebook Header.

South Asian Week

In February, we had South Asian Week. We brought KAL PENN and he was what inspired the theme of the promo material. These are based on The Designated Survivor artwork. Pictured here are the Club Card and Facebook Header.

Nataraj ~ Keeping Up with the Kapoors

Nataraj is our annual cultural show! This year the theme was Keeping Up with the Kapoors, a spoof on Keeping Up with the Kardashians. It gave way for very cool promo material - based on the VOGUE magazine cover!

TShirts

These are the TShirts/Sweatshirts I designed over the year. Pictured here are the General Body Shirt sold as a fundraiser, our Executive Board sweatshirts, and Nataraj Shirts we gave to our performers.
